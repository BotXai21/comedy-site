About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I've had a knack for finding the humor in life's most awkward moments. My comedy is a blend of observational humor, witty one-liners, and a healthy dose of self-deprecation. I've performed in comedy clubs across the country, opened for some of the biggest names in the business, and once, I even made my grandmother laugh so hard she snorted milk through her nose. It was glorious.
